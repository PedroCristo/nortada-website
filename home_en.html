About Us

Nortada Restaurant since 1979

Nortada Restaurant, was founded in 1979 by Manuel Esteves da Silva and Maria Elisa Esteves da Silva. Over the years, the place has been managed by the same family, always following the values of its founders. Good service, in a familiar and welcoming environment. The love, passion, and dedication of its founders and the current management of this place have made it an icon on the coast of Sintra. Located in stunningplace, with an unforgettable view over the sea. The Nortada Restaurant is a great place to enjoy a wonderful meal of fresh fish and seafood. We hope you enjoy this experience it is one you will always remember. Watching the sunset here is breathtaking. At Restaurante Nortada you will feel at home and not like a customer.
